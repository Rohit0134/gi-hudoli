About Us

Our school is dedicated to providing quality education and overall development of students. We focus on academics, sports,Technical filed And many type of skillsGIC HUDOLI was established in 2006 and it is managed by the Department of Education. It is located in Rural area. It is located in PUROLA block of UTTARKASHI district of Uttarakhand. The school consists of Grades from 6 to 12. The school is Co-educational and it doesn't have an attached pre-primary section. The school is N/A in nature and is not using school building as a shift-school. Hindi is the medium of instructions in this school. This school is approachable by all weather road. In this school academic session starts in April. The school has Government building. It has got 8 classrooms for instructional purposes. All the classrooms are in good condition. It has 2 other rooms for non-teaching activities. The school has a separate room for Head master/Teacher. The school has N/A boundary wall. The school has have electric connection. The source of Drinking Water in the school is Tap Water and it is functional. The school has 1 boys toilet and it is functional. and 1 girls toilet and it is functional. The school has a playground. The school has a library and has 100 books in its library. The school does not need ramp for disabled children to access classrooms. The school has 4 computers for teaching and learning purposes and all are functional. The school is having a computer aided learning lab. The school is Provided and Prepared in School Premises providing mid-day meal.

Academics

We offer classes from 6 to Class 12 with highly qualified teachers. Special focus is given to Science, Mathematics,amd art side group.
